Do's & Don'ts

During this theme my main focus was to improve my design skills, I have learned to pay attention to details. I learned that some design rules shall not be broken. For example, no borders on the cards or never use display fonts on UI elements. Its better to use sans serif fonts for body text and always having color contrast in mind.
