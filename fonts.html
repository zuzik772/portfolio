Fonts & UI cards

Theme 12c - Design for Developers

1. Font hierarchy

In this assignment I made a font hierarchy with 2 fonts: Azeret Mono, Poppins. For titles I have chosen within category displaay fonts to highlight the importance. I found monospaced typeface Azeret Mono very in line with my style. As per body font I chose Poppins beacuse as sans serif its very readable and offers many different styles.

2. Type Scale

I was undecisive between Major Third and Perfect Forth scale, but at the end I chose to use Major Third as it is more universal. I set the font sizes in rem units to keep my design responsive.

3. UI cards

The second part of the assignment was more challenging. First, I researched card design a lot and tried to find similarities and best practices. I learned to kill my darling – borders! Just no borders on the cards! But working with font weight, alignment and grouping made an impact on the final result.
